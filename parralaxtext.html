About

What do you get when you combine an obsession with visual design and an insatiable appetite for learning? You get me and the random projects I make.

My name is Olivia and I am a user assistance designer/songwriter/artist/ceramist/psychology enthusiast who loves to dance.

One day, I'll go back to SFU and finish my degree in Interactive Arts and Technology and Psychology. I love to listen and observe and understand the why, and the why behind that.

I have experience as a visual designer, ux researcher, and multimedia user assistance developer, where I combine my skills of visual information sharing with strong comprehension, writing and narrative abilities.

I believe design is a means to express, connect, educate and inform, and strive to use my power responsibly.
