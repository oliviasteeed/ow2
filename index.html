About

What do you get when you combine an obsession with visual design and an insatiable appetite for learning? You get me and the random projects I make.

My name is Olivia and I am a visual user assistance developer/information designer/SFU Interactive Arts and Technology and Psychology student. I love to listen and observe and understand the why, and the why behind that.

I have experience as a visual designer, ux researcher, technical writer, and multimedia user assistance developer, where I combined my passion for clear information design with strong comprehension, writing and narrative abilities.

I believe design is a powerful tool to express, connect, educate and inform, and am on a mission of using it to make complex things understandable.
